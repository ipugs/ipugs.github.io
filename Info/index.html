Rules:

  1. You must have a mic and be in mumble to play.
  2. New players, iron and steel players are all welcome, if you play silver you will need to offclass and take it easy.
  3. We're all here to get better so be able to take criticism and give it out to others in nice and constructive ways.
  4. Excessive off-classing (Pyro, Sniper, Engineer, etc. when not on last-holds) will not be permitted.
  5. Trash talking is ok, but to an extent. You will be given a warning on when you're taking it too far.
  6. Leaving, going afk, griefing and mic spamming are all ways to get you kicked/banned.
  7. Any player found cheating will be permanently banned.
  8. The admins/moderators word is final, you can make an appeal in our appeals form (linked below) rather then causing a scene in mumble.

Strike/Ban System:

  1. Being kicked from a pug (maximum of 3 times) for breaking any rule above will result in a 1-3 month ban depending on what they were for.
  2. Each kick counts as 1 strike out of the 3 strikes you have.
  3. After your ban you may return to our pugs, if you are kicked 1 more time you will be banned for a minimum of 3 months.
  4. If you would like to challenge a ban or strike you can post in our appeal thread with all information and our admins will decide what action to take.

How to join:

  1. Join the Mumble and pug channel specified in the announcement.
  2. Get in the game server using the Mumble comment.
  3. Wait until we have 12 players.
  4. Captains will play Medic.
  5. Get in spectator if you are not a captain.
  6. Medics will ubersaw fight at mid for first pick.
  7. Once you get picked, join your correct team and the admin will move you into the correct mumble channel.
